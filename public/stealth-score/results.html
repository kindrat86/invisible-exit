Methodology

The Stealth Score is a proprietary 12-factor methodology developed from 14 months of firsthand experience building anonymous micro-SaaS revenue while employed. Each factor maps to a documented risk vector in corporate side-business detection.

Data sources: 127+ documented cases of corporate employees building side businesses (self-reported via the Invisible Exit member community + public case studies). Not a random population sample — self-selecting group of employed professionals actively building side income. This biases scores upward (these are people who've already taken some steps). The general population of corporate side hustlers likely scores significantly lower.

Limitations: Self-reported data. Scores are best understood as relative risk indicators within the methodology, not as absolute safety guarantees.
