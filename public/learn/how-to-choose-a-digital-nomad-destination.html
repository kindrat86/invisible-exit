How to Choose a Digital Nomad Destination

Seven factors to evaluate when choosing a digital nomad destination: visa accessibility, cost of living, internet quality, timezone overlap, healthcare, safety, and community.

Seven factors to evaluate before choosing your next digital nomad destination: (1) Visa accessibility — how long can you stay legally? (2) Cost of living — PPP-adjusted for your income level. (3) Internet quality — minimum 50 Mbps for remote work. (4) Timezone overlap — at least 3 hours with your team. (5) Healthcare — quality and cost. (6) Safety — crime rates and political stability. (7) Community — existing expat and nomad networks.
