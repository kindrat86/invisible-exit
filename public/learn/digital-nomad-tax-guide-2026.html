Digital Nomad Tax Guide 2026

The complete tax guide for digital nomads and remote workers in 2026. Covers territorial tax systems (0% on foreign income), the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE up to $126,500), foreign tax credits, double taxation treaties, and the best countries for each tax strategy.

Tax systems compared

  • Territorial tax: Pay tax only on local income — 0% on foreign income. Best options: Panama, Costa Rica, Georgia, Malaysia.
  • FEIE (US citizens): Exclude up to $126,500 of foreign-earned income. Requires physical presence test (330 days outside US) or bona fide residence.
  • Foreign Tax Credit: Offset US tax liability with taxes paid abroad. Works for any amount, but requires paying local tax first.
  • Tax treaties: Avoid double taxation via US bilateral tax treaties with 60+ countries.
