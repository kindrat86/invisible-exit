Tax Optimization for Remote Workers

Five legal tax optimization strategies for remote workers and digital nomads: territorial tax systems, FEIE, foreign tax credits, tax treaties, and corporate structuring.

Five legal strategies to optimize your taxes as a remote worker: (1) Move to a territorial tax country — pay only local tax, not worldwide income tax. (2) Use the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE) — US citizens can exclude $120,000+ of foreign income. (3) Leverage foreign tax credits — offset US tax liability with taxes paid abroad. (4) Use tax treaties to avoid double taxation. (5) Consider corporate structuring — an offshore company with proper substance. Always consult a tax professional.
