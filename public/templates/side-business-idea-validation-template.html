Side Business Idea Validation Template

A template for stress-testing a side business idea before investing build time.

Format: Markdown / PDF · Free to use · Adapted for side business launchpad workflows

What's included

Problem

  • Target customer described in one sentence
  • Pain point is acute and frequent
  • Customers are actively paying for inferior solutions
  • Willingness-to-pay confirmed with 5+ prospects

Market

  • TAM estimate (top-down and bottom-up)
  • At least 3 direct competitors exist (validates market)
  • Clear angle to differentiate
  • Niche small enough to be defensible

You

  • You have relevant skills or network
  • You can build the MVP in 4-8 weeks
  • You can serve first 10 customers personally
  • Time commitment sustainable alongside day job

Decision

  • Proceed to MVP build
  • Iterate on the idea
  • Shelve and pick another
