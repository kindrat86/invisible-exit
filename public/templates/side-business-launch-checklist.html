Side Business Launch Checklist

A checklist for employed professionals to launch a side business without risking their day job.

Format: Markdown / PDF · Free to use · Adapted for side business launchpad workflows

What's included

Validation

  • Idea validated with 10+ prospect conversations
  • Willingness-to-pay confirmed
  • Competitive landscape mapped
  • Clear differentiation identified

Build

  • MVP scoped to 4-week build
  • No proprietary tech from employer used
  • Domain and brand not conflicting with employer
  • Working hours kept separate

Legal

  • Employment contract reviewed for side-business clauses
  • IP ownership clearly separated
  • Business entity formed if needed
  • Tax implications understood

Launch

  • First paying customer acquired
  • Revenue tracking set up
  • Customer support channel live
  • Monthly review cadence established
