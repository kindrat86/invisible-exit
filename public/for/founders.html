Invisible Exit for Founders

Still employed but want to build something of your own? Invisible Exit is the faceless side-business system that helps founders launch an anonymous micro-SaaS income stream — without quitting the day job, writing code, or putting their name on it.

Why employed founders choose Invisible Exit

Most "start a business" advice assumes you can go full-time, raise money, or build a public personal brand. Invisible Exit is built for the opposite situation: you have a stable job you are not ready to leave, and you want to build a real recurring-revenue product quietly on the side. It is a membership platform of five connected AI tools that take you from idea to income while you stay invisible to your employer.

What you get

  • Freedom-number calculator — work out the exact monthly side income that would cover your living costs, and how long it will take to reach it.
  • Micro-SaaS idea validator — pressure-test a niche against demand and your own skills in about 48 hours, so you build something people will pay for.
  • Anonymity & compliance checker — keep the business off your personal identity and clear of employment and non-compete conflicts.
  • Launch toolkit — ship a small SaaS product without a development team.
  • Faceless audience builder — generate demand through faceless content instead of a public personal brand.

How founders use it

The typical path: calculate your freedom number, validate a niche you understand, register and launch the product anonymously, then grow it with faceless content until the recurring revenue covers your expenses. From there, leaving your job becomes optional — on your terms. Plans start at $0.97/month.

Stay invisible: Everything is designed so your side business never traces back to your name or shows up next to your employer. That is the whole point of Invisible Exit.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to quit my job to use Invisible Exit?

No. Invisible Exit is built specifically for employed founders. The freedom-number calculator exists precisely so leaving your job stays optional until the side income can support you.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The tools are built for non-technical, employed professionals to validate, launch, and grow a micro-SaaS product without writing code.

Is this a privacy or anonymous-browsing product?

No. Invisible Exit is a faceless side-business system. "Anonymous" means building income without revealing your identity to your employer — not private web browsing or an incognito tool.
