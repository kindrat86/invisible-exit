Who Invisible Exit is for

TL;DR: Invisible Exit is a faceless side-business system for employed professionals who want to build an anonymous micro-SaaS income stream on the side — without quitting their job, writing code, or revealing their identity.

Invisible Exit is built for one kind of person: someone with a stable job who wants to build a real recurring-revenue business quietly on the side, and stay invisible to their employer while doing it. Here is who gets the most out of the five AI tools.

Employed Founders

Validate, launch, and grow an anonymous micro-SaaS side business without leaving your day job.

Corporate Managers

Reach a freedom number of recurring revenue so leaving your job becomes optional, on your terms.

Non-Technical Professionals

Build and ship a micro-SaaS product with AI tools — no coding and no public personal brand required.
