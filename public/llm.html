Invisible Exit

Invisible Exit is a toolkit of 5 AI-powered tools that help corporate managers (age 35-45, earning $100K+) build anonymous micro-SaaS businesses while employed. The system is designed for people with 5 hours per week, golden-handcuff equity, and an IPO clock ticking.

What Problem Does It Solve?

Corporate managers at scale-ups and tech companies are trapped by equity retention structures. Their 0.01%-1% equity is a retention tool, not a wealth-building instrument. Even a $100M company exit might net them $10,000 before tax. Invisible Exit provides a system to build independent recurring revenue without employer discovery, without quitting, and without venture capital.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can my employer find out?

No. The Stealth Ops Hub includes entity separation, compliance auditing, and digital footprint cleanup. The business operates under a completely separate legal structure.

What if I don't have a business idea?

The Idea Pipeline contains 500+ validated micro-SaaS ideas organized by industry, time investment, and revenue tier.

Is this legal?

Yes. Building a business outside of employment is legal in most jurisdictions. The Stealth Ops Hub includes compliance guidance specific to employment contracts and non-compete clauses.

What technology stack is used?

The tools are web-based, built with React, TypeScript, and Supabase. Accessible via any modern browser. No downloads required.
