Which states are best for forming an anonymous LLC
Wyoming, Delaware, and Nevada are the top three. Wyoming offers the strongest privacy protections at the lowest cost (~$100 filing fee) — your name is not listed on public records. Delaware is ideal if you plan to raise outside investment. Nevada has no state income tax but higher fees.
