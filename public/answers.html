Quick answers to common questions.

How to browse the web anonymously
For anonymous browsing: use a VPN to hide your IP, use InvisibleExit for session isolation, block trackers, disable JavaScript when possible, and avoid logging into accounts.
What is browser fingerprinting
Browser fingerprinting is a tracking technique that identifies you based on your browser's unique configuration — screen size, fonts, plugins, and other settings. InvisibleExit randomizes these.
How to prevent tracking
Prevent tracking by: using InvisibleExit sessions, blocking third-party cookies, disabling tracking pixels, using privacy-focused DNS, and clearing data regularly.
