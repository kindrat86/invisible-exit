Micro-SaaS

A software-as-a-service business targeting a niche market, typically run by 1-3 people with low overhead.

In depth

Micro-SaaS businesses focus on solving specific problems for a well-defined audience. They thrive on low customer acquisition costs and high retention. The key advantage is that a solo founder can build and maintain them without a team.
