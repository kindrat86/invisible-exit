Common Questions

What is the Stealth Score?

A proprietary 12-factor methodology that rates how safe your side business is from employer detection. Each factor is scored 0-100; the composite is a weighted average reflecting real risk exposure. Based on research into 127+ corporate side-business cases.

How accurate is this?

The methodology was developed from 14 months of firsthand experience building anonymous micro-SaaS revenue while employed. Each factor reflects a real legal, technical, or operational risk vector. It's a diagnostic tool, not legal advice — but every factor maps to a documented corporate exit strategy.

Why doesn't my employer already know?

Employers typically don't run active surveillance on employees' side businesses. However, poorly structured side businesses leave digital breadcrumbs — public WHOIS records, LinkedIn cross-references, payment processor paper trails, and social media overlaps — that can surface during background checks, IP audits, or internal investigations.

What happens after I get my score?

Each factor in your breakdown links to a specific playbook in The Invisible Exit Method — concrete steps to improve that dimension. Members get access to the full Stealth Ops & Compliance tool that automates the fixes.
