Frequently Asked Questions

How does InvisibleExit protect privacy?

InvisibleExit creates isolated browsing sessions that automatically destroy history, cookies, cache, and tracking data when you close the session.

Is InvisibleExit like Incognito mode?

Incognito mode only prevents local history storage. InvisibleExit also blocks tracking pixels, prevents fingerprinting, and destroys all session data on exit.

Does InvisibleExit hide my IP address?

InvisibleExit focuses on session-level privacy. For IP hiding, use a VPN. The two tools complement each other.

Can websites detect InvisibleExit?

InvisibleExit randomizes browser fingerprints to make detection difficult. However, no privacy tool is 100% undetectable.

Is InvisibleExit free?

InvisibleExit has a free tier for basic use and paid plans for advanced features. Visit the pricing page for details.
