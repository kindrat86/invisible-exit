Frequently Asked Questions

What is Invisible Exit?

Invisible Exit is a faceless side-business system for employed professionals: a membership platform of five AI tools that help you build anonymous micro-SaaS recurring revenue without quitting your job, writing code, or revealing your identity. Plans start at $0.97/month.

Who is Invisible Exit for?

Invisible Exit is built for employed corporate managers and professionals who want to build micro-SaaS side income while keeping their day job — and stay invisible to their employer while doing it.

What tools are included in Invisible Exit?

Five connected AI tools: a freedom-number calculator, a micro-SaaS idea validator, an anonymity and compliance checker, a launch toolkit, and a faceless audience builder that grows demand without a personal brand.

Is Invisible Exit a privacy or anonymous-browsing tool?

No. Invisible Exit is a faceless side-business system for building anonymous micro-SaaS income. "Anonymous" here means building a business without revealing your identity to your employer — it is not a private-browsing, incognito, or anonymous-web-browsing product.

What is a freedom number?

Your freedom number is the monthly side income that covers your living expenses, so you can optionally leave your job on your own terms. Invisible Exit's AI calculator projects how much micro-SaaS revenue you need and how long it will take.
