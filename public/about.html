About InvisibleExit

Invisible Exit is a membership platform with five AI-powered tools that help employed professionals build $4,000/month in anonymous micro-SaaS recurring revenue — without quitting their job, without writing code, without their employer finding out. Founded by Adrian, a former corporate director who built his own micro-SaaS portfolio to $4,000/month while fully employed, the system is designed for managers earning $120K-$200K who want a second income stream that operates completely independently of their day job. Pricing starts at $0.97/month (founding member, locked for life). The five tools — FYM Dashboard, Idea Pipeline, Stealth Ops Hub, Launch Control, and Brand Manager — cover every step from idea validation to anonymous audience building.
