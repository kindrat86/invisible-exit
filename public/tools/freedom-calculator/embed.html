Add a free, interactive Freedom Number Calculator to any website with a single line of code. Your visitors calculate when their side business replaces their salary — it's a value-add they'll share.

Quick embed (iframe)

Copy this into your HTML where you want the calculator to appear:

<iframe src="https://invisibleexit.com/tools/freedom-calculator" width="100%" height="900" frameborder="0" style="border:none;max-width:900px;margin:0 auto;display:block" title="Freedom Number Calculator"></iframe>

How it works

When you embed the calculator:

  • Your readers get a free, genuinely useful tool — they'll stay on your page longer
  • You get increased engagement time (Google loves this)
  • We get a backlink from your site (which helps us rank — thank you!)
  • Everyone wins. The calculator is 100% free with no upsells inside the embed

Customize

Pre-fill the calculator with your audience's typical salary by appending URL params:

https://invisibleexit.com/tools/freedom-calculator#s=125000&m=2000&f=single

s = salary, m = current MRR, e = business expenses, l = living expenses, f = filing status (single/married)
