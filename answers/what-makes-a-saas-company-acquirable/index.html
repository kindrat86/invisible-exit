What makes a SaaS company acquirable?

Answer: Three factors: recurring revenue above $500K ARR, a defensible moat (proprietary tech, network effects, or brand), and clean financials with 80%+ gross margins. Invisible Exit scores you against all three.

Acquirers look for: (1) Revenue quality — is it recurring, diversified across customers, growing? (2) Moat — can a competitor replicate this in 6 months? (3) Cleanliness — are there legal, technical, or financial skeletons? Invisible Exit's readiness score breaks down your position on each dimension.
