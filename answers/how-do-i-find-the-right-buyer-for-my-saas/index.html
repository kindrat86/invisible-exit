How do I find the right buyer for my SaaS?

Answer: Invisible Exit's buyer matching uses your product category, tech stack, revenue profile, and growth rate to match against a database of 2,000+ active SaaS acquirers — from strategic buyers to PE firms to individual operators.

The matching engine considers: strategic fit (does your product fill a gap in their portfolio?), technical compatibility (can they integrate your stack?), financial fit (does your ARR/profile match their acquisition criteria?), and geographic/customer overlap.
